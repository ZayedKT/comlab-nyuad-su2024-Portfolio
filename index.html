30MFF Website

Individual
27 May 2024

Heading To Class is a dedicated website for the 30 Minute Film Festival (30MFF), showcasing the artistic essence of the Arts Center at NYUAD through a student's journey to their first class. It highlights diverse elements of art, including drawings, handicrafts, interior design, and architecture, all found within the Arts Center. The design of the website, in addition to a 90-second video and an interactive image carousel, provides an engaging and immersive digital experience to the users. This multimedia approach allows users to appreciate the creativity and detail found within the several elements of art.

Interactive Comic

Teamwork (Shahzaib Hassan, Muhammad Ahmed, and Reginald Appiah-Sekyere) 30 May 2024

Shazly The Bachelor is a website aimed to convey the message that hard work ultimately pays off. Using the story of an individual called Shazzy who is graduating high school, the user gets to make decisions on future decisions that Shazzy should make. There are four different paths that the user can choose and therefore four different versions of Shazzy’s story. The use of different storylines in the comic allows the user to investigate the different storylines and ultimately understand the moral of the story, hard work ultimately pays off and is the reason behind having a successful career..

Smart Water Bottle Holder

Teamwork (Serikbayev Nurmukhambet, Aaviskar Paudyal, and Fadel AlShamsi) 20 January 2023
